Aqua Fly®

Aqua Fly is an intelligent rescue drone designed to detect and assist people at risk of drowning. It provides rapid surveillance and fast emergency response operations over water environments.

Mission Showcase

236,000 people die from drowning worldwide every year.

We're here to change that.

AquaFly is a solar-powered smart drone that tracks people on the beach and sends a signal to rescuers in case of a threat of drowning, and flies to the scene and throws an automatic rescue bagel at the victim.

Our Design

Aqua Fly features a custom hexagonal airframe engineered for stability over water. The lightweight yet durable structure allows rapid deployment and precise maneuverability during rescue operations.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of engineers and developers work hands-on to build, test, and refine the Aqua Fly system — combining expertise in drone technology, marine safety, and embedded systems.
